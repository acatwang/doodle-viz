Online Scheduling Behavior Reflects Cultural Differences

The differences are time perception, cultural values, and group decision-making. They all have to do with your pace of life, individualist/collectivist societies, and how they plan their schedule....

Scatterplots Correlation Coefficient Matrix

Here are the things we looked at...

IDV: The indivisualism score of the country.
        The higher, the more indivisualism the country is.
Pace of Life:
        The overall pace of life og the country.
         It includes walking speed, postal speed, and clock accuracy.
Planning Ahead of Time:
         Average advance planning time in minutes across all polls.
Response Time:
         The average response time per country.
Fraction of Consensus:
        The fraction of the polls that reaches a consesus meeting time among all participants.
GDP:
         A country's GDP per capita.
PDI:
         Hofstede's Power Distance scores for each country.
Number of Options:
         Average number of options provided by each participants for each country.
